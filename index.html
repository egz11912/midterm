Tasty Pizza Recipe


Ingredients


  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cornmeal
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps


  1. Mix the dough ingredients together by hand or use a han-help or stand mixer.
  2. Knead by hand or beat the dough with your mixer
  3. Place dough into a greased mixing bowl, cover tightly, and set aside to raise for about 90 minutes.
  4. Punch down risen dough to release air bubbles. Divide in 2.
  5. Roll dough out into a 12-inch circle. Coverand rest as you prep the pizza toppings.
  6. Top with favorite pizza toppings.
  7. Bake pizza at a very high temperature for only about 15 minutes.
